How it Works

  • 1. Convert Watts to Power Cells to start the 24-Hour countdown.

  • 2. When the countdown has ended:

    • a) convert your Watts to Power Cells to increase your production or

    • b) sell your Watts for ETC.

  • 3. You can always sell or convert your Watts before the countdown ends which will reset the countdown.
